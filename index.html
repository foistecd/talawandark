What is this?

This is a fork of the Talawanda Web Portal that was abandoned by the Talawanda School District in 2020 in favor of Abre.

Adopting Abre was a horrible mistake on Talawanda's part. This made navigating to the school's most used websites far more annoying. Abre is also a privacy hazard by disclosing what you do on their website to third parties.

Help Thanos-snap Abre by bookmarking the TalawanDARK Web Portal on your device and using it instead of Abre.
